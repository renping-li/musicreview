Mayer Hawthorne

A Strange Arrangement (2009, Stones Throw Records) [Soul] ★★★☆
Eleven love songs from a proud Detroit native. "I Wish It Would Rain" is obviously a tribute to the Temptations. My favorite line is "She lives on the other side of town / And I know that her father doesn't want me hangin' around", which I think is a less obvious tribute to the Shangri-Las, "Whatcha mean when ya say that he came from the wrong side of town?".
